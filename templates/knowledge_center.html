Lifespan refers to the total number of years a person lives, while healthspan is the number of years lived in good health without chronic diseases or disabilities. SwasthBharath aims to increase both, with special focus on extending healthspan through technology-enabled preventive care.

AI in healthcare enables early disease detection, personalized treatment plans, predictive analytics for health risks, automated medical imaging analysis, drug discovery acceleration, and improved clinical decision-making through data-driven insights.

Each life stage has specific health priorities: prenatal care for mothers and babies, growth monitoring for children, mental health for adolescents, chronic disease prevention for adults, and comprehensive geriatric care for elderly. SwasthBharath provides targeted programs for each stage.

Digital health services are accessible through government health platforms, telemedicine apps, digital health records systems, and IoT-enabled health monitoring devices. Contact your local health center or visit our help section for guidance on accessing these services.
